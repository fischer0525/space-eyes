Technology

Satellites utilized by Space-Eyes have what is known as Phased Array Structure. This allows us the ability to steer radar beams by providing different scanning modes. The nature and advantage of RADAR satellites over Optical is that RADAR satellites can see 24/7, at night, through cloud cover and during inclement weather.

Satellite Imaging

Current satellite AIS providers only offer data captured from a shipboard sensor. Our satellite AIS offers data captured from a shipboard sensor AND is simultaneously validated by the same satellite radar. Our satellites also offer different scanning modes ranging from "stripmap" which provides resolution up to 6m over a swath of 15 - 20km, to "maritime" mode which is ideal for large sea regions by providing resolution from 6 - 13m over a swath of 400km.

Data Analysis
