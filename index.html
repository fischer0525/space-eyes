Offering Radar Satellites as-a Service

Mission

Aggregate and exploit RADAR satellite & maritime data to deliver valuable and timely new perspectives in the global maritime domain.

Team Talks

Illuminating the Blue Economy

Our oceans provide the planet's trade routes.

Over 90% of world trade is carried by sea in 250,000+ merchant ships/crafts registered to over 150 countries. This is the Blue Economy that has asset exceeding US $20 trillion.

We use Artificial Intelligence + Business Intelligence to deliver risk modeling and actionable information in the maritime domain. Easily access vast amounts of relevant information through one web-based portal.

Difference

SAR + AIS + Maritime Business Intelligence via Subscription.

We provide the world’s first commercial S-band satellite-based synthetic aperture radar(SAR) correlated with ships’ Automatic Identification System (AIS)
We aggregate our proprietary SAR and AIS data in near-real time. Our users can correlate information from over 50 security, safety, and maritime sources.
We have embedded sophisticated Artificial Intelligence technology to dramatically enhance threat evaluation.
We provide a maritime-focused Business Intelligence suite of risk modeling and Geospatial visualization tools within the portal at Space-Eyes
