Capt. Jatin S. Bains
CEO

Jatin has 35+ years’ maritime experience from ship Captain to CEO. In this lead role he brings domain knowledge on maritime, logistics and supply chain security.

He is recognized as the creator of automated maritime threat evaluation with a patent on eLogistics which led to the company’s CATE™ system. He is an active participant in the global maritime security community, represented the USA at APEC 2006 on maritime security and is fluent in 5 languages.

Brent Baker
Data Architect

Vice President

Software Architect

Maj (Ret) Yong Pong HWA
