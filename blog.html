Oct 2 2017
Space-Eyes enters into a Memorandum of Understanding with General Dynamics to offer Radar Satellite as-a Service to the US Government and Coalition allies. The radar satellite data downloaded in the United Kingdom shall be ingested into an analysis platform powered by its proprietary CATE system hosted on the Amazon cloud. The stakeholders will be able to access the portal via a secure web browser at www.Space-Eyes.co.uk

May 03 2016
Space-Eyes and its affiliate enter into an NDA with CAE Brunei Multi-Purpose Training Center (MPTC) Sdn Bhd to collaborate and develop Radar (SAR) Satellite Operations and ground based data download services. The CAE Brunei MPTC facilities are a joint venture between CAE Canada and the Government of Brunei Darussalam (Ministry of Finance) and ideally suited for a Satellite Ground Station near the equator with a landing point on the Asia-America Gateway cable.
